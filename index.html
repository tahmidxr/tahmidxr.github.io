- About Me -

Hi, I'm Tahmid, an aspiring engineer with a passion for technology and creativity. Currently, I’m preparing for my engineering admission while working on exciting projects like building an IR receiver and sender using ESP8266. My interests also extend to graphic design, where I enjoy blending aesthetics with functionality to create visually appealing and meaningful designs. I’m deeply fascinated by emerging technologies like cryptocurrency and blockchain, as well as the innovative use of tech in storytelling—particularly in shows like Alice in Borderland. Exploring how creativity and technology intersect is what drives me in both my projects and designs. When I’m not designing or tinkering with gadgets, I love listening to music on my trusty AirPods, learning about health and personal growth, or engaging in meaningful conversations. Feel free to connect if you share similar interests or have something inspiring to discuss!
